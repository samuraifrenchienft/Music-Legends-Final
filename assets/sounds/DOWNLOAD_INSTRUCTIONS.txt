AUDIO FILES NEEDED - Download Instructions
==========================================

This bot requires 4 audio files to provide premium audio feedback.

QUICK DOWNLOAD LINKS (Free & Legal):
------------------------------------

1. legendary_pull.mp3
   - Visit: https://freesound.org/search/?q=epic+reveal
   - Download any epic orchestral hit or fanfare (2-4 seconds)
   - Rename to: legendary_pull.mp3

2. daily_claim.mp3
   - Visit: https://freesound.org/search/?q=coin+sound
   - Download a coin/cash register sound (1-2 seconds)
   - Rename to: daily_claim.mp3

3. card_pickup.mp3
   - Visit: https://freesound.org/search/?q=whoosh
   - Download a soft whoosh or pickup sound (0.5-1 second)
   - Rename to: card_pickup.mp3

4. pack_purchase.mp3
   - Visit: https://freesound.org/search/?q=cash+register
   - Download a purchase/success sound (1-2 seconds)
   - Rename to: pack_purchase.mp3

ALTERNATIVE: Use YouTube Audio Library
--------------------------------------
https://studio.youtube.com/channel/YOUR_CHANNEL/music

Filter by:
- Duration: 0-30 seconds
- Mood: Bright, Happy, Dramatic (depending on sound)
- Genre: Sound effects

ALL SOUNDS MUST BE:
- MP3 format
- Royalty-free / Creative Commons
- Under 8MB
- Placed in this directory (assets/sounds/)

The bot will work without these files, but users will not hear audio feedback.
